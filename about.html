Our Story

Founded in 1992, JVM Consultancy began with a vision to transform the real estate landscape of Noida and the surrounding regions. At the heart of our journey is a steadfast commitment to transparency, integrity, and dedication—values that have guided our growth from a modest consultancy into a highly trusted name in the real estate industry. Our founder, Prayag Chaudhary, set out with a singular goal: to create an empowered real estate experience where clients receive expert guidance and personalized service tailored to their unique needs. Over the years, JVM Consultancy has successfully helped thousands of clients, from homeowners to large-scale commercial developers, navigate the complexities of property buying, selling, renting, and constructing. Throughout our three decades of growth, we have continuously adapted to evolving market trends, expanding our range of services to meet our clients’ changing demands. With over 500,000 satisfied customers and a 98% satisfaction rate, JVM Consultancy has become synonymous with trust, reliability, and results in the real estate space. As we look to the future, our commitment remains the same: to make real estate transactions seamless, rewarding, and efficient. Mission

To provide exceptional real estate solutions by upholding transparency, integrity, and dedication, ensuring that every client finds the ideal property to meet their needs. We aim to simplify the buying, selling, and renting process through expert guidance and personalized services, fostering long-lasting relationships built on trust and satisfaction.

Vision

To be a leading real estate service provider known for delivering innovative and reliable property solutions across India. We envision creating a seamless real estate experience that helps individuals and businesses achieve their goals, contributing to the development of sustainable and thriving communities.
